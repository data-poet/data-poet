### About Me

I am an Analytics Engineer with experience across the full data lifecycle, from ingestion and transformation to analysis, visualization, and deployment. I work primarily with Scala, Spark, Python and SQL, focusing on building and maintaining data pipelines, ETL processes, and data governance frameworks. My role is centered on engineering reliable and scalable data solutions while acting as a bridge between business and technical teams, translating requirements into robust data products that support decision-making.
